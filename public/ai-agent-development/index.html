What is an AI Agent?

An AI agent is a smart program that can make decisions, carry out jobs, and learn from experience. It works on rules, goals, or learning models. These agents are not just chatbotsthey can be thinkers, doers, or helpers.

There are many types:

Reactive agents:
respond without memory

Learning agents:
get smarter over time

Collaborative agents:
work in groups

Autonomous agents:
work without humans

At Deligence, we build AI agents tailored to your business goalswhether you need a reactive, learning, or autonomous system. As your trusted AI agent development services, we help you choose the best fit.

Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs)

Which company is making AI agents?

Many tech companies are making AI agents. Some big ones are OpenAI, Google, Meta, Microsoft, and Amazon. But smaller AI agent development companies (like ours!) i.e Deligence Technologies build custom agents for businesses too.

How can I build my own AI agent?

You need the right tools like ChatGPT or LangChain, some data, and a clear goal. If you don't code, you can work with an AI agent development company to build one for you.

How to make money from AI agents?

You can sell AI agents, offer them as a service, or use them to improve your business (like saving costs or growing sales). Some people even sell chatbots in app stores or use them in online stores.

How much does it cost to build an AI agent?

It depends! Simple agents may cost a few thousand dollars. Bigger or smarter ones cost more. We offer different packages to fit different budgets.

Can I sell AI agents?

Yes, if you build an AI agent or have rights to one, you can sell it to othersjust like selling software or apps.

Can I create an AI of myself?

Yes, it's possible! You can create an AI that talks like you or answers questions the way you would. It uses your voice, words, or writing style.

What do AI developers do?

They teach computers how to understand, talk, think, and solve problems. They build programs that make smart choices using data.

What is the main goal of AI?

The goal of AI is to help machines think and act smartso they can help people work, learn, and solve real-world problems faster.
